About Anno 117 Production Calculator

This free Anno 117 production calculator helps you plan and optimize production chains. Calculate building requirements, view production times, and visualize dependency graphs for all goods in Anno 117: Pax Romana. Perfect for planning your empire's economy and maximizing efficiency.

Features

  • Interactive Production Chains: Visualize complete dependency graphs for all Anno 117 products
  • Building Calculator: Calculate exact building counts needed for target production rates
  • Aqueduct Support: Factor in productivity bonuses from Aqua Arborica and Field Irrigation
  • Recommended Ratios: Get optimal building ratios for balanced production
  • Cost Analysis: View total building costs and maintenance requirements

Note: Not all production chains implemented (Missing Albion and Patricians)

Tip: On touch, drag with one finger to pan and pinch to zoom. On desktop, hold right mouse button to pan and use the mouse wheel to zoom.
